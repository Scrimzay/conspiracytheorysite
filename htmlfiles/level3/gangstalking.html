THIS REFERS TO THE IDEA THAT INDIVIDUALS ARE BEING FOLLOWED, STALKED AND HARASSED BY A SECRET GROUP
OR ORGANIZATION DUE TO THE FACT THE INDIVIDUAL HAS INFORMATION OR KNOWLEDGE OF SOMETHING THEY SHOULDN'T AND THUS
ARE BEING STALKED TO INTIMIDATION TO PREVENT THAT INFORMATION FROM GETTING OUT OR TO SILENCE THE INDIVIDUAL.
GANGSTALKING VICTIMS TEND TO RECOGNIZE RECURRING STRANGERS IN THEIR AREA OR VICINITY THAT ARE MARGINALLY OUT OF PLACE
THAT ALLOW THEM TO BE IDENTIFIED AS THE STALKERS.
