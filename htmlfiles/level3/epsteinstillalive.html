THIS REFERS TO THE IDEA THAT JEFFREY EPSTEIN IS ACTUALLY STILL ALIVE. THIS THEORY IS BIRTHED FROM THE IDEA THAT
EPSTEIN DIED UNDER SUCH COINCIDENTAL CIRCUMSTANCES THAT HE COULDN'T HAVE POSSIBLY KILLED HIMSELF AND AT MOST THAT HE
WAS KEPT ALIVE BUT EXILED AND PUT SOMEWHERE AND EXCHANGE FOR NOT SAYING ANYTHING. FURTHER CLAIMS ARE THAT THE BODY CLAIMED
TO BE HIS HAD DIFFERENT HEAD FEATURES, MAINLY HIS EARS, THAT SHOW TO PROVE HE IS STILL ALIVE AND THAT THE CORONER THAT INSPECTED
HIM TO PROVE HIS DEATH WAS PAID OFF.
