THIS REFERS TO THE IDEA THAT THE GOVERNMENT ACTUALLY HAS WEAPONS TO ORBITAL SPACE WEAPONS, SPECIFICALLY WEAPONS
THAT THEY USE TO WIPE OUT THEIR ENEMIES WITHOUT BEING FOUND OUT. SUCH LASER WEAPONS DO EXIST ALREADY ON EARTH AND ARE
ACTIVELY USED BY POLICE FORCES AND MILITARIES HOWEVER IN THIS INSTANCE, THE GOVERNMENT (SPECIFICALLY) THE
US MILITARY HAS THESE TYPES OF LASERS IN SPACE. THE LASERS ARE MEANT TO SHOOT MICROWAVES, PARTICLE BEAMS
AND SOUND BEAMS.
